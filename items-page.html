When purchasing any stationery item, you’ll be guided through a series of design-related questions before you add the item to your cart. These selections establish a starting point for the collaboration that will occur after you place your order, as we work together to personalize your items.

Within each design, there are a number of variable elements that we will modify and customize while working together, and there are also a number of unchanging features, outlined here.

Constant Features

We view the floral artwork as a signature element that makes every item within each collection distinct. For this reason, we’re unable to make specific modifications to the illustrations or switch artwork between different items within our catalogs. In addition, each collection has a specific typographic palette comprised of a select grouping of typefaces, which cannot be substituted between collections.

  • Flower types & colors
  • Floral Arrangement

  • Collection-specific typefaces
