A Love Letter

from
Venamour

There’s a certain enigma bound to the “third finger”, companion to the pinky and bearer of the engagement ring. A curious expression, “Vena Amoris”, tells of the origins of the ring finger tradition. Latin for “the vein of love”, the myth describes the illogical presence of a single vein that was said to connect this finger exclusively to one’s heart. This fallible logic awarded the fourth digit its symbolic privilege, and makes for an oddly romantic fable. Wedding lore and fictional heartstrings aside, at Venamour, we do think there is some elusive truth to a connectedness between the hands and the heart.
