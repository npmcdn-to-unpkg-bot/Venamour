OLGA VEISBERG is a fine jewelry brand founded by Olga Veisberg.

  • Every OLGA VEISBERG piece is one of a kind, we do not work on made-to-order basis.
  • Precious and semi-precious gemstones are the focus of the design.
  • The pieces are designed and produced by skilled artisans in Kyiv, Ukraine.
Continue reading

Olga Veisberg is a successful businesswoman and a jewelry enthusiast with a deep knowledge of the philosophy behind it.

She opposes conventional approaches to jewelry: “Do not be afraid to mix everything together – silver, gold, bijoux. If the jewelry suits you, wear it however you like. There are no forbidden combinations.”
